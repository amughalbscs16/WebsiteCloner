FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I become a reseller?

Fill out our contact form and we will provide you a demonstration. This will allow you to familiarize yourself with our SDKs and provisioning APIs.

What kind of resellers does WLVPN work with?

WLVPN works with various resellers, from small businesses looking to create an internal privacy product, to larger hosting and cloud-based companies looking to expand their software offerings.

Can I use my own logo and designs in the software?

Yes, we encourage you to do so. As part of the WLVPN service, youâ€™ll receive a set of graphics which you should apply your own branding to.

Can you skin our apps for us?

If you are unable to skin our apps yourself, we can provide simplified app versions that we brand for you.

Will you handle payment processing for my customers?

No. WLVPN is a reseller service, and as an individual reseller, you are responsible for collecting fees from your customers. The pricing model is entirely up to you.

Will you provide support to my customers?

No, like payment processing, resellers must provide their own customer support. Should you encounter any service issues, WLVPN does offer tier 2 reseller support.

How do I manage renewals, cancellations and other customer needs?

As a reseller, your primary interface with the service will be through the APIs and control panel. Typically, routine signups and cancellations are handled via API from your merchant site, and one-off actions will be performed by you in our control panel.
